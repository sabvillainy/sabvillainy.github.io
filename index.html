About Me

Hi there! My name is Sabri Yılmaz. I am a Computer Programming student at Izmir University of Economics.

Currently, I am enrolled in an Associate's degree program with the goal of transitioning into a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering through Turkey's "Dikey Geçiş Sınavı (DGS)". This transition is my primary academic objective as I aim to deepen my knowledge and expertise in the field.

My journey into the world of programming began with a curiosity for how software works, and it has since evolved into a deep commitment to learning the art of coding and problem-solving.

I have a keen interest in software development, game design, and AI technologies. My dream in my software development career is to create a widely-used, appreciated, and popular program that people love.

I am familiar with several programming languages, including Java, C#, SQL, HTML and CSS. Additionally, I am currently expanding my skill set by learning the Spring Framework and JavaScript.

As a programmer, I believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork. I enjoy working with diverse teams to bring ideas to life, leveraging each team member's strengths to achieve common goals. My ultimate aim is to contribute to projects that have a meaningful impact, whether they are academic, professional, or personal endeavors.

In addition to my technical skills, I am a strong advocate for continuous learning and improvement. I regularly engage with the latest industry trends, tools, and best practices to ensure that my knowledge and skills remain current and relevant in the fast-paced field of technology.

Currently, I am eager to apply my skills and enthusiasm in an internship position to contribute to innovative projects and further my professional development. If you're interested in hiring me, check out my resume.


Hobbies

In my spare time, I have a deep passion for music and gaming. I play electric guitar, bass guitar, and drums, and I am an active member of my university's music group. My love for music extends beyond playing instruments; I also have an extensive music collection, including vinyl records, CDs, and cassettes, which I treasure.

I have been a computer game enthusiast since childhood, with a particular fondness for RPGs. Gaming has always been a significant part of my life, providing both entertainment and a way to connect with others who share similar interests.

Like many, I enjoy watching movies, TV shows and reading books during my free time. Recently, I have developed a strong interest in films, especially those considered cult classics. When it comes to reading, I am drawn to classic novels and philosophical works, finding both to be incredibly enriching and thought-provoking.

These hobbies not only provide relaxation but also fuel my creativity and broaden my perspective, contributing to my overall personal and professional growth.
